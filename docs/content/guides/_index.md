Each guide is built around a job rather than a single command. They assume you
have run the [quick start](/getting-started/quick-start/).

- [Browse the front page](/guides/browse-the-front-page/) and turn it into a
  reading queue.
- [Read a thread](/guides/read-a-thread/) and pull out the comments you want.
- [Search and filter](/guides/search-and-filter/) with tags, dates, and points.
- [Follow a user](/guides/follow-a-user/) and list their submissions.
- [Pipe hn into scripts](/guides/scripting-with-jq/) with JSONL and `jq`.
